Drones

Hermes

  • PayLoad5 kg
  • Range10KM
  • FlightHours14hrs

Hermes is our most economic, and versatile drone yet. Be it delivering a personalised medical order to your doorstep, or transporting an emergency medical kit to the scene of an accident, you can rely on Hermes to get it done. It packs a modular quadcopter design, and is loaded with our most advanced image detection system yet. Capable of travelling 4 km on a single charge, carrying a maximum load of 8 kg. It is ideal for use in urban areas. This sturdy drone has a carbon fiber build and it's container uses our indigenous deployment system. The entirity of Hermes is designed in house, and will be fully operational by May of next year.

Apollo

  • PayLoad5 kg
  • Range10KM
  • FlightHours14hrs

Apollo is our most ambitious drone yet. Envisioned to be a green drone, that draws its' power from the sun having virtually limitless flight. Apollo is truly, the drone of the future. Apollo is in its very early design stages, and we expect to start working on it from September 2020..

Zeus

  • PayLoad5 kg
  • Range10KM
  • FlightHours14hrs

Zeus is our most powerfull drone yet. It bulds upon the architecture of the Hermes. Hosting a lager, more powerfull battery along with a more powerfull octa-propellor system. Zeus can carry heavier payloads and travel a farther distance. Currently in the planning and design stage..
