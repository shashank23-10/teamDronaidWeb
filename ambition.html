Our Ambitions

filter_1

Aim

The central ambition of Project DronAid is to enhance the accessibility and streamline the healthcare system. This goal is being realized by establishing an intelligent network of UAV systems that are dedicated to medical applications. Through the integration of Artificial Intelligence into Unmanned Aerial Vehicles and the development of corresponding applications, our aim is to facilitate practical clinical applications and emergency services at the community level.

filter_2

Mission

We are diligently working on creating efficient autonomous payload delivery systems primarily focused on medicines and healthcare essentials. Concurrently, we are developing an accompanying app that will serve as the interface for our comprehensive medicinal delivery services.

filter_3

Competitions

We are also in the process of designing drones for our flagship event, UAS '24, which takes place at BMFA Buckminster, UK. In addition, we will participate in various national and inter-college competitions.
